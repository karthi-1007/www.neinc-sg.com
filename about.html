About US

From Concept to Creation, Comprehensive Approach to Industrial & Manufacturing.

N&E Engineering Pte Ltd is an Engineering company established in 2014. We provide comprehensive solutions catered for clients’ needs and have since expanded in sectors such Oil & Gas, Food & Beverages, Process, Pharmaceutical, and Waste Water Treatment industries. Our core expertise lies in designing, building, and installing fluid transfer systems, clean-in-place systems, liquid mixing and dispensing, Scrubber Dust Collection systems as well as providing space management through 3D modeling.
